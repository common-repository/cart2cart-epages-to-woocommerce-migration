== Description ==
**Supported WooCommerce versions: 1.1.x – 1.6.x, 2.x, 3.x**

Forget about tedious manual csv import / export - a new robust store is a matter of 3 simple steps with Cart2Cart. The service performs automated data transfer from ePages to WooCommerce and guarantees to keep your data safe. Full migration lasts a few hours, its duration depends on the number of entities you migrate.

To set up the migration requires no tech knowledge and is easily performed with the help of Online Migration Wizard. Responsive Support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any issue you might face. Most importantly, your current ePages store will not be affected during the transfer. 

**Cart2Cart: ePages to WooCommerce Migration plugin is free to download, and after installation you will be able to proceed with the 2 migration options:**

1. Free Demo migration — a limited amount of entities will be migrated to your live WooCommerce store (or Cart2Cart test store) in less than 30 mins. Demo migration helps to test the quality and reliability of service in advance.
1. Full Migration — the UNLIMITED amount of eCommerce data with all the corresponding relations will be transferred from ePages to WooCommerce.The minimal price for Full Migration  starts at $69 and may increase depending on the number of entities to be transferred and additional options chosen.

### What data can be moved during ePages to WooCommerce migration 

**Products** - Name, SKU, Short Description, Full Description, Manufacturer, Tax Class, Sort Order, Special Price, Meta Title, Meta Keywords, Meta Description, Product Image, Additional Images, Quantity, Stock Availability, Weight, Width, Height, Options (Name, Price), Product Variants (SKU, Length, Weight, Attributes, Width, Height, Quantity, Images, Price, Special Price, Special Price From-To Date).
**Product categories** - Name, Status, Meta Title, Meta Description, Meta Keywords, Category Images.
**Manufacturers** - Name.
**Customers** - Customer Billing Address .

==Installation==

1. Download the plugin.
1. Log in your WordPress admin panel
1. Go to Plugins > Add New > Browse. Pick Cart2Cart plugin and click “Install Now” button. 
1. Activate it by clicking “Activate plugin”.
1. Find Cart2Cart plugin on the left side menu. Register Cart2Cart account and proceed with the migration setup.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do you migrate multiple languages? =
Yes, Cart2Cart supports migration of multiple languages from ePages to WooCommerce providing a paid WPML module is installed and configured on your WordPress before the data transfer. Please, go <a href="https://www.shopping-cart-migration.com/faq/45-woocommerce/385007-is-multilanguage-migration-to-from-woocommece-possible"> here. </a> for more info.

=Does migration from ePages to WooCommerce will influence the speed of my current store?=
The actual migration process will not  influence the speed of your current store performance.
As an exception, you might experience website performance slowdown for one of the following reasons that Cart2Cart is not capable either to control or prevent:
- the amount of products exceeds memory limit of your hosting plan;
- wide range of third-party modules;
- etc.
To get more details - go <a href="https://www.shopping-cart-migration.com/faq/10-general-questions/196-does-migration-influence-the-speed-of-my-store"> here. </a>
= Can I transfer my metadata to WooCommerce? =
Cart2Cart supports metadata migration to WooCommerce. Online store owners can migrate their meta titles, keywords and descriptions from ePages to WooCommerce store, but they need additional WordPress plugin for it.
For more detailed information on metadata migration to WooCommerce go <a href="http://www.shopping-cart-migration.com/faq/45-woocommerce/302-can-i-migrate-metadata-to-woocommerce"> here. </a>
